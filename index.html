Welcome

This website is home to Ultimate Fighting Chickenship, a game made by Tempête Group. Here, you will be able to find information about the project and the team, a link to download UFC as well as various links and resources related to the creation of the game.

What is Ultimate Fighting Chickenship?

Ultimate Fighting Chickenship is a fighting game similar to the likes of Street Fighter and Mortal Kombat.
The final goal for the project is to make a parody of existing staples of the fighting genre, hence the name being an obvious reference to the Ultimate Fighting Championship mixed martial arts promotion company.
The character you control is a humanoid chicken with different abilities and combos. Whether you use fireballs or combos of kicks and punches to defeat your opponent is up to you!
